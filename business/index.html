What is it

A survey that finds the drink someone will like.

PROOF asks guests a few quick preference questions, then turns those answers into a drink recommendation. It helps people move from "I have no idea what I want" to a confident order.

Who it's for

For curious guests and undecided drinkers.

It is built for people who want to try something new, people who do not know the cocktail menu well, and anyone who wants a recommendation that feels personal instead of random.

Why restaurants care

Better drink choices create better business signals.

Restaurants can learn what kinds of drinks guests are asking for, which flavor profiles are gaining traction, and where the menu has unmet demand.

More confident orders

Guests are more likely to buy a drink when the recommendation matches what they already like.

More useful menu data

Preference patterns can reveal which styles, ingredients, and categories customers want most.

More reasons to return

A better drink experience makes the night feel more personal, which gives guests another reason to come back.
